The purpose of the Chicana/Latina Foundation (CLF) Scholarship Program is to assist Chicana/Latina students in completing their undergraduate and graduate college education.

Toward this end, the CLF awards $1,500 scholarships and the accompanying leadership training on a competitive-basis to continuing full-time undergraduate and graduate college students of Chicana/Latina heritage.

Occasionally, funders and individual philanthropists donate funds to be given in addition to the $1,500 scholarship for targeted achievements.

Award decisions are made by the Board of Directors of the Chicana/Latina Foundation based on the recommendations of volunteers from the community.

  1. Each submission is read and rated by volunteers.
  2. From the higher-rated submissions, semi-finalists are selected.
  3. Each semi-finalist is interviewed by a panel of volunteers.
  4. The Board of Directors selects finalists based upon the recommendations of the interview panels.

Applicants understand that the scholarship is given to those who can and want to attend the Leadership Institute (LI). LI is a retreat offered solely for winners of our scholarship and is held in 2 parts.

The first one will be in Early August 2014 and the second sometime in early February 2015.

All winners of the scholarship are expected to attend both sessions. We are giving advance notice of the dates, so you can prioritize attending these retreats. Failure to participate may affect eligibility for our scholarship program.
